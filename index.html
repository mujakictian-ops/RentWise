FAQ

Questions

How is RentWise different from Stessa or Baselane? +
Stessa and Baselane track your income and expenses. They show you what happened. RentWise tells you what to pay the IRS and when — based on your full tax picture including your salary, deductions, depreciation, and state.
Do I still need a CPA? +
For complex situations (1031 exchanges, LLC structuring) a CPA is still useful. But RentWise handles the quarterly estimated tax piece that most CPAs don't remind you about between April appointments — and generates the Schedule E that makes your CPA faster and cheaper.
What if I have a regular job AND rental income? +
This is exactly where most landlords go wrong. Rental income stacks on top of your salary and pushes you into a higher bracket. RentWise calculates the combined rate — so you're not over or underpaying.
